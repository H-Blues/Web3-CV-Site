📑 Research and Publication

The Application of Deep Reinforcement Learning in Finance

Principal, supervised by Prof. Kong Yan

I studied the daily trading strategy of the portfolio of two virtual currencies (Bitcoin and Ethereum) and did penetrative research on the Financial Markov Decision Process (FMDP). Designed a deep reinforcement learning algorithm based on Deep Q Network, and solved the problem of quantitative trading strategy. Random noise on the currency price with a threshold setting was also used to simulate some sharp price changes.

Publication: Deep Q Network Applied in Trading Portfolios of Virtual Currencies https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-981-19-6901-0_117

Detection of Diseases Disaster of Apple Leaves based on Vgg & Yolov4

Leader for implementation and planning, supervised by Prof. Han Ying

To identify the diseases of apple leaves, a voting model composed of deep learning YOLO-v4 and VGG-16 was designed. The results show that the YOLO-v4 model used for the two iterations of the voting model achieves an average recognition accuracy of 87.30%, while the VGG16 model with two iterations achieves an average recognition accuracy of 83.38%. The voting model composed of the above four models has the recognition accuracy of three plant diseases, which is 92.06%.

Publication: A Voting Model for Apple Disease and Pest Identification Based on Deep Learning DOI: 10.12325/j.issn.1672-5336.2022.09.021
